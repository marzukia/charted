Examples

Integer Interval

axis_tick_interval=3

Shows every 3rd label (Jan, Apr, Jul)

Percentage String

axis_tick_interval="25%"

Shows 25% of labels (2 out of 8)

Float Proportion

axis_tick_interval=0.25

Shows 25% of labels (same as "25%")
